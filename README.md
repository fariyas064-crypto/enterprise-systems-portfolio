Enterprise System Project Portfolio 
This repository serves as my individual portfolio for the Course. It documents my understanding of the system being developed by my team and highlights my role and contributions throughout the project. 
The problem that's being solved: Currently the NlU website struggles to have a stable section to provide students with enough information to register for their courses. Students are unable to choose for themselves. Our project focuses on creating a system that helps improve organization, communication and efficiency. 
System Vision: The goal of this system is to have a different section in D2L to just register for your courses and have enough information to navigate throughout credits required for your degree. 
My role on the team: In this project I contribute to planning,m documentation and collaboration with my teammates. I partcipate in system discussions, help organize project ideas and take care of the work where AI is needed and do the final review before submitting each work.
